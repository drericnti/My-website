Eric Abui

IP Networking Expert | IT Solutions Architect | Agile enthusiast

Eric Abui is an experienced Network Specialist with a strong background in communication engineering. Holding both a Master’s and Bachelor’s degree in Communication Engineering from the University of Science and Technology Beijing, Eric’s career has been defined by his innovative approach to network optimization.

Starting as a Network Operations Trainee at Camtel in Cameroon, he quickly rose to key roles at Huawei Technologies, managing global teams across the Middle East and Africa. At the Royal Bank of Canada (RBC), Eric spearheaded network automation projects, using tools like Ansible and API calls to enhance system efficiency. With certifications including CCNA and AWS Cloud Architect Associate, he combines technical expertise with leadership to drive innovation.
