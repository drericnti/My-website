Eric Abui

Eric Abui is an experienced Senior Network Analyst with a strong foundation in communication engineering. Holding both a Master’s and Bachelor’s Degree in Communication Engineering from the University of Science and Technology Beijing, Eric’s passion for network optimization and technology has been the driving force behind his career in the telecommunications and IT industries.

From the beginning of his career, Eric has demonstrated exceptional technical expertise. Starting as a Network Operations Trainee at Camtel in Cameroon, he quickly gained hands-on experience in network monitoring, troubleshooting, and deployment. This foundation paved the way for his subsequent roles at Huawei Technologies, where he held key positions across various global offices.

At Huawei, Eric’s leadership skills shone through as he led teams to support cutting-edge technologies in IP, transmission, and wireless networks. As a Team Leader and CTO in Bahrain, he was the frontline customer interface, ensuring seamless operations and timely resolution of technical issues. He managed complex network infrastructure and played a crucial role in maintaining the integrity of the systems, which spanned multiple regions, including the Middle East and Africa.

Eric’s career reached new heights when he joined the Royal Bank of Canada (RBC) as a Senior Network Analyst in Toronto. At RBC, he has been instrumental in managing and enhancing network automation, working with global teams to ensure the reliability and scalability of the bank’s network infrastructure. His work includes implementing network improvements, performing regular maintenance, and driving innovations like automating network operations using tools such as Ansible and API calls for KPI output. His proactive approach to system manageability and problem resolution has earned him recognition as a key asset in RBC's network operations.

With an in-depth understanding of routers, switches, firewalls, load balancers, and network devices, Eric has earned numerous certifications, including Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA), Cisco Certified Specialist in Enterprise Core, and AWS Cloud Architect Associate. His technical skills, combined with a Project Management Professional (PMP) certification, make him a versatile and strategic contributor to any team.

Fluent in French, English, and Mandarin, Eric thrives in multicultural environments and brings a global perspective to his work. His drive to stay ahead in the rapidly evolving world of networking, coupled with his technical and leadership expertise, continues to fuel his passion for innovation and excellence.
